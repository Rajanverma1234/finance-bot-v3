=== PERSONAL LOANS ===
Q: Personal loan ke liye kya documents chahiye?
A: Aadhar card, PAN card, 3 months salary slip, 6 months bank statement, aur passport size photo chahiye.

Q: Personal loan ki interest rate kya hai?
A: Personal loan ki interest rate 10.5% se 18% per annum hoti hai, aapki credit score ke hisaab se.

Q: Personal loan kitne time mein milta hai?
A: Documents complete hone ke baad 24-48 ghante mein loan approve ho jaata hai.

Q: Maximum kitna personal loan mil sakta hai?
A: Maximum 25 lakh rupaye tak personal loan mil sakta hai.

=== HOME LOANS ===
Q: Home loan ke liye minimum salary kya honi chahiye?
A: Home loan ke liye minimum 25,000 rupaye monthly salary honi chahiye.

Q: Home loan ki interest rate kya hai?
A: Home loan interest rate 8.5% se 11% per annum hoti hai.

Q: Home loan ka tenure kitna hota hai?
A: Home loan 5 saal se 30 saal tak ke liye milta hai.

=== CREDIT CARDS ===
Q: Credit card ke liye apply kaise karein?
A: Online portal par jaayein, form fill karein, documents upload karein. 7 working days mein card mil jaata hai.

Q: Credit card ka bill pay karne ki last date kya hoti hai?
A: Statement date ke 20 din baad due date hoti hai.

Q: Credit card limit kaise badhayein?
A: 6 months regular payment ke baad limit badhane ke liye request kar sakte hain.

=== FIXED DEPOSITS ===
Q: FD ki interest rate kya hai?
A: 1 saal ki FD par 7.5% aur 2-3 saal par 8% interest milta hai.

Q: Minimum FD kitni kar sakte hain?
A: Minimum 1000 rupaye se FD shuru kar sakte hain.

Q: FD tod sakte hain before maturity?
A: Haan, premature withdrawal possible hai lekin 1% penalty lagti hai.

=== GENERAL QUERIES ===
Q: Account kaise kholein?
A: Nearest branch mein jayein ya online portal par jaayein. Aadhar, PAN aur photo chahiye.

Q: Net banking activate kaise karein?
A: Branch mein request form submit karein ya customer care call karein.

Q: ATM card block kaise karein?
A: Customer care call karein ya net banking se block kar sakte hain turant.
